New Jersey policymakers are considering a Carbon Fee and Dividend policy to decrease fossil fuel pollution while protecting the economy.

The policy puts a price on carbon emissions and returns the revenue evenly to NJ citizens as a monthly check. The check helps offset the energy cost increases.

This calculator estimates the effect of the policy on your bottom line.
